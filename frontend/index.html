My Dream Vacation: Santorini, Greece

White-washed houses, blue domes, and breathtaking sunsets.

About the Place

Santorini is a beautiful island in the Aegean Sea, known for its iconic white-and-blue architecture, volcanic beaches, and dramatic views over the caldera.

Picture

Activities To Do

  • Watch the sunset in Oia.
  • Take a boat tour around the caldera.
  • Swim at the Red and Black sand beaches.
  • Enjoy local Greek food and wine.

Attractions To Visit

  • Oia village and its famous blue-domed churches.
  • Ancient ruins at Akrotiri.
  • Fira town and the cliffside walk to Oia.
  • Local wineries and vineyards.
